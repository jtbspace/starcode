    How to use Starfinder!

    Draw!

    You can draw the star pattern of a constellation, on the prediction area.

    Predict!

    Let Starfinder refine the dots and find the closest matching constellation.

    Results!

    See the result and facts about the predicted constellation. Don't be too harsh as the program is only 60% accurate.

     
    Draw the sky

    The image will be compared to 80+ constellation

    The result will appear with drawn constellation and facts.

    How Starfinder works

    Draw the star pattern of the constellation that you see. Starfinder will use a (Machine Learning) Deep Neural Network to find the best matching pattern against 14 of the most commonly visible constellation. The constellations are selected based on simplicity to draw and most visible in both hemispheres all year round!
